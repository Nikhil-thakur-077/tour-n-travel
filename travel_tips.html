Preparation & Culture

🛂

Visas & Documents

Most travelers need an e-Visa to enter India. Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your stay and carry multiple physical copies of your documents.

💊

Health & Safety

Drink only bottled or filtered water. Carry a basic first-aid kit and get recommended vaccinations well in advance. Travel insurance is highly recommended.

🙏

Cultural Etiquette

Always remove your shoes before entering temples and homes. Dress modestly, especially in rural areas and religious sites. A simple "Namaste" goes a long way!

💰

Money & Budgeting

While UPI and cards are widely accepted in cities, carry cash (Indian Rupees) for small vendors and rural areas. ATMs are widely available but may have limits.

📶

Connectivity

Get a local SIM card at the airport (Airtel or Jio are popular). Wi-Fi is common in hotels and cafes, but data is very affordable in India.

🚂

Transportation

For long distances, the Indian Railways offer an incredible experience—book in advance! In cities, use apps like Uber or Ola for reliable taxi services.

Best Time to Visit

India is vast, and the ideal time to visit depends on where you're headed. Here's a quick guide to help you plan.

❄️ Winter (Oct - March)

Best for Rajasthan, Kerala, and Goa. Perfect weather for sightseeing and beaches.

☀️ Summer (April - June)

Ideal for Himalayan regions like Ladakh, Manali, and Rishikesh. Beat the heat in the mountains.

🌧️ Monsoon (July - Sept)

Perfect for the Western Ghats and Kerala. The landscapes turn lush green and beautiful.
